Milk

Heritage & Lineage
"Through our sister concern Satnam Process Engineering, we have been manufacturing Milk Processing Plants since 1995 — bringing nearly three decades of continuous refinement to every project."

Comprehensive milk processing plants engineered for precise temperature control, hygiene, and operational reliability. Every system is designed around the client's specific throughput requirements and regulatory environment.

→ Temperature-Controlled Pasteurizers — Process heat exchangers maintain exact time-temperature profiles for HTST and LTLT pasteurisation with full automated control and alarm management.
→ Agitators — Carefully designed agitation systems ensure homogeneous mixing and temperature uniformity across the full tank volume without product damage.
→ Process Piping — All product-contact piping fabricated in 316L stainless steel, electropolished, slope-drained and CIP-validated to the highest hygienic standards.
→ Integrated CIP Systems — Fully automated clean-in-place circuits with chemical dosing, temperature monitoring, and rinse verification.
→ Separation & Standardisation — In-line separators and standardisation equipment to achieve precise fat content targets automatically.
→ Storage Silos — Insulated raw milk and pasteurised milk silos with continuous agitation and temperature monitoring, up to 300KL capacity.

02 · Buttermilk & Cream
Plant Type 02

Buttermilk &
Cream
Processing

Specialised processing systems engineered for the unique rheological and thermal properties of cream and buttermilk. Our equipment maintains the stability of cream-based products throughout the entire process chain.

→ Cream Base Stability — All equipment is designed to preserve and maintain the stability of cream-based products, with gentle handling to prevent fat phase disruption.
→ Tailored Equipment Configuration — Systems are custom-configured to each client's product mix, capacity requirements, and available utilities — no off-the-shelf compromises.
→ Weld Polishing to Hygienic Standard — All internal welds are dressed and electropolished to Ra < 0.8µm, eliminating bacteria harbouring sites and simplifying CIP.
→ Precision Temperature Management — Close-tolerance temperature control during pasteurisation, cooling, and storage to preserve product quality and shelf life.
→ Buttermilk Separation Circuit — Dedicated separation and standardisation loop for cultured buttermilk production with controlled acidification monitoring.
→ Fat Monitoring & Control — In-line fat content analysers linked to standardisation valves for real-time product specification control.

03 · Whey Processing
Plant Type 03

Whey
Processing
Plant

Engineered specifically for the demanding byproduct stream from cheese production — recovering maximum value from whey through precision crystallisation and concentration processes.

→ Cheese Byproduct Specialist — Plants designed specifically for the highly variable composition of whey from cheese production, handling both sweet whey and acid whey streams.
→ Crystallizer Tanks — Purpose-built crystallisation vessels with controlled agitation and temperature ramping profiles for maximum lactose crystal yield and purity.
→ Concentration & Evaporation — Multi-effect evaporation systems for pre-concentrating whey prior to crystallisation, optimising energy consumption.
→ Protein Recovery — Integrated ultrafiltration circuits for whey protein concentrate (WPC) and whey protein isolate (WPI) production.
