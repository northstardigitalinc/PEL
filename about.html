Who We Are

Built on
Precision

Perfect Engineering Limited was founded in 2023 with one uncompromising ambition: to deliver precision-engineered stainless steel solutions that set a new benchmark for quality and reliability in process engineering.

Based in Canada with manufacturing capability through our sister concern Satnam Process Engineering in India, we combine the engineering rigour of a Canadian operation with the fabrication depth of an Indian manufacturing base, giving our clients the best of both worlds.

Our team of 80+ engineers and specialists brings decades of collective experience across the dairy, food, pharmaceutical, beverage, chemical, and brewery sectors. We don't just build equipment. We build the infrastructure that powers industries.

Our Story

The Journey
So Far

From a vision for better process engineering to a growing international operation, built on expertise, relationships, and relentless commitment to quality.

2018
The Foundation

Satnam Process Engineering established in India, bringing together a team of experienced process engineers and fabricators with deep expertise in dairy and food processing equipment.

2020
Growing the Portfolio

Expansion into pharmaceutical and chemical sectors, broadening our technical capabilities and bringing GMP-compliant manufacturing expertise into our growing portfolio.

2022
International Expansion
